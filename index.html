Ganna Glass.
Logo design

Ganna Grudnytska is an artist – a glass maker and an illustrator with a style of her own: organic shapes combined with a geometric touch. When I was asked to make a logo for her I took inspiration from her fascination for nature and design. The logo itself forms a hidden signature as there’s a lot of “Gs” in her surname. The branding is to be used on the website, glass prints, business cards, and any other media – it’s simple and universal.

Tailor shop
brand collateral

Teresa Urbanek is a tailor from Wrocław, running her own small business in the Nadodrze district. As Pantograf collective (me and Justyna Dybala), we were asked to build a new visual identity for her shop. So a new branding was created: logo, custom typography, leaflets, business cards, labels. A very important part of this collateral is a hanging signboard designed by us and made by Kujto – metal workshop.

EABS
logo design

EABS is a Polish septet playing sounds inspired by traditional Polish jazz as well as by other genres, like hip-hop, jungle, funk, and experimental music. Logo is being used on record covers, in promotional material, and banners.

Lasek
visual identity

Lasek is a small vegetable and fruit shop. Its owner Mrs Grażyna is a true nature lover. She spends every vacation in the forest fishing and picking up mushrooms. In Polish “Lasek” means “little forest” – and that’s where the name comes from. New design was applied to both the shop window and to the signboard which constitutes a main part of its visual identity.
